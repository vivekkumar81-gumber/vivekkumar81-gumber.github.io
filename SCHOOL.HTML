About Us

Shaping Futures in
Dhingan Wali, Punjab

Govt High School Dhingan Wali is a government-run educational institution managed by the Punjab Government and affiliated with the Punjab School Education Board (PSEB), Mohali. The school is committed to providing quality, inclusive education to students from Class 6th to 10th.

🏛️

Infrastructure

Our Facilities

Well-equipped facilities to support learning, health, and overall development of every student.

🏫

Spacious Classrooms

Well-lit, ventilated classrooms equipped with blackboards and adequate seating arrangements for all students.

📚

School Library

A rich collection of PSEB textbooks, reference books, and reading materials to encourage a love for learning.

⚽

Playground

Computer Education

Basic computer education and digital literacy programs to prepare students for the modern technological world.

Clean Drinking Water

Safe and clean filtered drinking water is available throughout the school premises for students and staff.

Sanitation Facilities

Separate, hygienic washroom facilities for boys and girls, maintained to ensure a clean and safe environment.

Latest Updates

Announcements & News

Stay informed about important school notices, examinations, and upcoming events.

Notice 📅 March 10, 2025

Annual Examination Schedule 2024–25

Annual examinations for Classes 6th to 10th will commence from April 1, 2025. Students are advised to collect their admit cards from the school office by March 25.

Exam 📅 February 20, 2025

Class 10 Board Exam Preparation Camp

A special revision camp for Class 10 students will be held every Saturday from March 1 to March 29, 2025. Attendance is compulsory for all Class 10 students.

Event 📅 January 26, 2025

Republic Day Celebration

Republic Day was celebrated with great enthusiasm at school. Students performed cultural programs and the principal addressed the gathering. Prize distribution ceremony was held.

Holiday 📅 March 5, 2025

Holi Holiday Notice

The school will remain closed on March 14, 2025 on account of Holi festival. Classes will resume on March 15, 2025 (Monday). Wishing everyone a colourful celebration.

Notice 📅 March 1, 2025

New Textbook Distribution

PSEB textbooks for the session 2025–26 will be distributed to all students starting March 17, 2025. Parents are requested to ensure their children attend school on that day.

Event 📅 February 5, 2025

Annual Sports Day 2025

The Annual Sports Day will be held on March 22, 2025. Students are encouraged to register for their preferred sports events with their respective class teachers by March 10.
